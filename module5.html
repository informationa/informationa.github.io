Blockchain Ecosystem

A Blockchain Ecosystem catering Construction 2.0

PACT designs and builds blockchain based systems and infrastructure for the construction industry. Unlike traditional digitalisation, PACT products are distinguished by its complete offering data security and system wide trust through comprehensive data privacy, authenticity, and confidentiality design.

  • Electronic Contract Procurement System - eCPS
  • Universal Digital Works Supervision System - uDWSS
  • a blockchain-based Building Information System - BIM

Anybody can do digitalisation and system design, yet very few results in systems that truly address data privacy, authenticity, and confidentiality as a whole. PACT not only leverages blockchain technology but also the most comprehensive governance models and policies along with world leading protocols to build a truly complete digital trust ecosystem.
